 Requirements for Blood Donation 

 Donor should be in good health.

 Age between 18 and 65 years.

 Weight not less than 50kg.

 Donor will undergo preliminary interview.

 Not recommend for pregnant or lactating women.

 Eat a healthy meal, avoiding fatty foods like hamburgers,ice-cream.

 Wear a shirt with sleeves that you can roll up above your elbows.

 Relax, listen to music, talk to other donors or read while you donate.

 Drink an extra 16 oz. of water before your appointment.
